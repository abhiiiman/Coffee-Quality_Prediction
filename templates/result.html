What are Category 2 Defects?

Category 2 defects in coffee quality refer to specific types of imperfections or irregularities found in coffee beans during inspection or grading. These defects may include, but are not limited to, insect damage, black or sour beans, under-ripe or over-ripe beans, or other abnormalities that affect the overall quality and taste of the coffee.
